Process / Five studies

The work behind
the finished surface.

Each chapter uses supplied workshop footage. The films are short, muted and secondary to the object itself.

01 / Setting

Position before pressure.

Each stone is placed, checked and secured without losing the geometry around it.

02 / Adjustment

Millimetres become light.

Small corrections determine alignment, security and the way a stone returns light.

03 / Assembly

Parts become a form.

Individual settings and decorative elements begin to read as one coherent object.

04 / Polish

Light returns.

Edges and surfaces are brought to a controlled finish without losing their definition.

05 / Finish

The final inspection.

The process ends with a human decision: whether the object now feels complete.
